Connecting with your mind...

Being in control of your mind is an essential part in meditation, it allows you to free your mind and de-stress from any troubles you may have. Different meditation's can enable you to take back control of the mind and enable you to learn a way in which you can better deal with emotions that aren't serving you a purpose. De-conditioning your mind can take time, but with consistency, it can transform you. If this something that connects with you, please sign up!

Connecting with your body...

Your body is a vital part of your being, it allows you to freely live life in a way which honours you. Nourishing your body and taking special care with it is your way to say 'thank you'. Yoga is a great tool to connect your mind and body, it allows you to move, stretch and balance in ways which benefit your overall health and mental wellbeing. It is known to aid in physical pain including but not limited to arthritis, flexibility, back pain and heart health. Yoga allows you to bring awareness to your body and mind, focusing on your breathing and tuning into your thoughts. If this something that connects with you, please sign up!

Top tips for beginners...

Here at Pure Souls, our mission is to assist you in your journey to reaching whatever your mind and body is asking from you. Whether it is practicing self love, kindness, mental and body strength, we are here for you. Here are some of our favourite tips to start you off on the right path. Create your own peacful space. Put aside phones or anything that could potentially make you become distracted. Sit with your legs crossed, back straight, head facing up at eyebrow level and your palms resting on each knee facing outwards. If you are unable to do any of these steps, it is completely fine. You can lay, or sit with your back supported, with your eyes open. It is your practice, you can do whatever you feel serves your body and your mind. Take your time. This is your moment.
