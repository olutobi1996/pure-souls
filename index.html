Connecting with your mind...

Being in control of your mind is essential in meditation, being able to focus your attention allows you to free your mind and de-stress. Also being in control of your mind allows you to control any emotions and un-condition yourself and become the awarness who is the observer of your thoughts, doesnt that sound interesting?

Connecting with your body...

Body is a vital part of a human being, Our body is truly a temple, it is a machine that needs taking care of. Yoga allows us to improve flexibility which then improves back pain, artharitis and any heart health. If you are someone who struggles to sleep, look in no over place then to start yoga! would you like to know more?

Top Tips...

Here at Pure Souls we have everything you need to become an all round healthier, vibrant, liberated human being. Here is some little tips to help you on your way.. Create your own peacful place away from phones or noise anything that could make you become distracted. Sit leg crossed, head looking slightly up with your eyes closed. Focus on looking inbetween your eyes when closed. If you cant do any of that dont worry you can stand, lay or even have your eyes open. Its your practice and your time.
